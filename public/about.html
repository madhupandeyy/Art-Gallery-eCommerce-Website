Who We are



Madhu



Madhu is the visionary behind our gallery. With an innate appreciation for diverse art forms, Madhu plays a crucial role in curating exhibitions that captivate and inspire. Their keen eye for detail and commitment to showcasing emerging talents make Madhu an integral part of our team.

Lakshitha



Lakshitha, our resident art enthusiast, brings boundless energy and creativity to the team. Their dedication to fostering a welcoming environment for artists and visitors alike ensures that every exhibition at Art Gallery is a memorable experience.

Rajashekar



Rajashekar, our tech-savvy member, manages the digital presence of our gallery. From designing the website to leveraging social media, Rajashekhar ensures that the world gets a glimpse of the incredible artworks featured at Art Gallery.

Jaydev



Jaydev, our logistical mastermind, handles the behind-the-scenes operations with precision. From coordinating exhibition logistics to managing communications, Jaydev ensures that everything runs smoothly, allowing our visitors to immerse themselves in the world of art.
